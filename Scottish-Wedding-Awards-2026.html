Scottish Wedding Awards 2026: Glasgow Ceremony Delivers Big Wins & Venue Spotlight!

Hot off the press from today’s glamorous black-tie event at the Marriott Hotel in Glasgow — the 13th Scottish Wedding Awards 2026 (by Oceanic Awards) celebrated Scotland’s elite wedding talent.

2026 Awards Night
Scottish Weddings Awards 2026

Scotland’s Wedding Industry Takes Centre Stage

Finalists were revealed back in November after public votes, and winners were announced live tonight—honouring excellence in venues, suppliers, and service across the country.

“These awards shine a spotlight on Scotland’s historic estates, countryside venues, and creative suppliers dominating 2026 wedding trends.”

Fife Dominates the Awards

  • Exclusive Wedding Venue of the Year: Rufflets (St Andrews)
  • Romantic Wedding Venue of the Year: Cambo Estate (Kingsbarns) and Balbirnie House (Glenrothes)
  • Wedding Co-ordinators of the Year: Cambo Estate
  • Fife recorded an impressive 22 finalist businesses, making it one of Scotland’s strongest wedding regions for 2026/2027 couples.

Creative Suppliers & Industry Trends

  • Cake designers including Lynne’s Cake Emporium and Maria’s Baking Stories.
  • Florists such as Brodie and Bloom’s and Poppy, alongside photographers Lifetime Photography and Dougi McMillan.
  • Bridal boutiques including Kudos Bridal and Mirka Bridal Couture, plus specialists like Cragganmore Ceilidh Band and Jack Webster DJ.
  • These wins reflect rising demand for intimate, twilight celebrations and outdoor ceremonies enabled by relaxed 2026 wedding laws.
